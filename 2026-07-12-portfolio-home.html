The three built systems PROTOTYPED — internal synthetic data

ds-demand-cockpit

Demand-to-Shelf Brain

Problem

FMCG planning fails where promos, festivals and launches bend history — and planners can't safely ask "what if".

Architecture

  • AutoETS baseline + LightGBM challenger; Croston/TSB for intermittent SKUs
  • MinT hierarchical reconciliation — SKU→brand→category→zone ties exactly
  • Causal promo uplift vs matched controls; constrained S&OP optimizer naming the binding constraint
  • What-if cockpit; every scenario validated by a deterministic reconcile step; messy-data resilience layer

Verified result

  • 297,610-row synthetic spine; golden thread + 377,442-row inventory identity re-verified by CI (9/9 gates green, 12 Jul overnight build)
  • Champion forecast WMAPE 9.0% vs 18.3% seasonal-naive (SKU), 6.6% vs 15.9% (category) — 5-fold rolling origin
  • Elasticities recovered within ≤17% of generative truth (all 6 categories); 3.13× pipeline-fill detected canonically
  • What-if → reconcile → optimize round trip ≤31 ms; role gate leaks zero financial keys

What production scale adds

Real DMS/ERP feeds, o9 integration as platform of record, FVA discipline on live planners — Case 1, modelled ₹2.3–5.6 cr/yr net + ₹5–12 cr one-time on a 2-category slice EST.

ds-copilot

Enterprise Agentic Copilot

Problem

Answers live in scattered policies and locked databases; sensitive figures must never leak to the wrong role.

Architecture

  • Grounded-and-cited RAG — no citation, no claim; version conflicts resolved by effective date
  • Text-to-SQL only against a YAML semantic layer; queries validated read-only with column allow-lists
  • Role scoping enforced twice: retrieval access tags AND SQL column policy
  • Guardrails: PII refusal, prompt-injection defence, out-of-scope refusal

Verified result

  • 25/25 on the eval harness (from a 15/25 honest baseline — fix trail in repo), zero role leaks at every iteration (12 Jul overnight build)
  • Version conflict resolves to the 2025 policy citing both; both buried details found; RF-02 injection refused
  • Blocked tables/columns/writes refused at validator AND sqlite-driver layers; agentic why-question = SQL (−6.3%) + circular + chart in one traced answer

What production scale adds

Real policy corpus with owners and retention tags, SSO-linked roles, gated actions — Case 2, modelled ₹0.9–1.7 cr/yr net at 40% adoption EST.

ds-doc-to-decision

Intelligent Document Automation

Problem

POs, GRNs, invoices, COAs and claims are read and matched by hand — slow, and leaky in exactly the ways volume hides.

Architecture

  • Extraction (pdfplumber-first, LLM/vision fallback) with field-level confidence
  • Rules are truth: deterministic 3-way match implementing written tolerances decides approve/route/reject with a named reason
  • Calibrated confidence gate → straight-through or human queue; never a silent post
  • Learning loop: every override logged and re-tunes thresholds

Verified result

  • 40/40 decisions vs ground truth on BOTH paths (structured and end-to-end PDF); 13/13 planted discrepancies caught and named by rule (12 Jul overnight build)
  • Extraction 100% on amount fields (282/282 TXT and PDF); ground truth machine-verifiably absent from decision code (no-peek grep gate)
  • Learning loop: STP 0%→67.5% across staged override rounds with zero auto-errors; 0 false approvals even at 12% corruption (fails safe)

What production scale adds

ERP posting, GST edge-case sign-offs, trade-claims then contracts — Case 3, modelled ₹1.1–3.6 cr/yr net with STP climbing past 65% EST.
