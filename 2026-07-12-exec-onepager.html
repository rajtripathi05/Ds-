The thesis

DS Group has publicly committed to doubling from ₹10,000+ crore (FY25) VERIFIED to ₹20,000 crore by its centenary year 2029 VERIFIED. That doubling will be won or lost in decisions made weekly across 5,000+ distributors, ~15 lakh directly-served outlets and 15+ own manufacturing sites VERIFIED. AI's job is not novelty — it is decision quality at that scale: forecast what the trade will pull, prove which rupee of trade spend works, and stop reading documents by hand. Management already names AI and automation as a growth enabler VERIFIED.

Governance before scale

Standing AI Council · DPDP-aligned data classification and consent handling · dual-layer access control (retrieval tags + SQL column policy — already demonstrated in the copilot prototype) · three human-in-the-loop tiers, promotion only on measured error rates · model cards, drift monitors, immutable decision logs · annual independent audit of anything that acts autonomously. Nothing automates until it beats the incumbent process in a measured shadow run.

The ask — three decisions

  1. Sponsor: name an executive sponsor and convene the AI Council (month 0).
  2. Access: grant a two-week data audit across DMS/ERP extracts to replace estimates with actuals and re-score the portfolio.
  3. Phase 0: approve three shadow-mode pilots — copilot, document automation, demand cockpit — at ₹0.9–1.4 cr, judged only against pre-agreed exit gates.
