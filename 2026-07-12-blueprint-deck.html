Thesis

Reach is the moat. Decision quality is the multiplier.

  • The asset: a distribution machine — 5,000+ distributors, ~15 lakh outlets served directly, 35 lakh+ total reach, 15+ own manufacturing sites V8 V9. AI's job is to make every node decide better and cost less to serve.
  • The route: three decision spines — demand→supply, knowledge→action, document→decision — chosen because they compound and because working prototypes already exist for all three P1–P3.
  • The posture: the announced o9 + PwC planning programme V13 is treated as the platform of record; this blueprint adds the causal, what-if and governance layers a platform rollout does not deliver by itself.
  • The discipline: governance before scale — nothing automates until it beats the incumbent process in a measured shadow run, and every claim in this deck is tagged and traceable.

Governance — the licence to automate

Five pillars, one council, three autonomy tiers

  • Privacy & DPDP: data classified before ingestion; purpose limitation; consent & grievance paths for consumer bots; no employee personal data in training corpora; on-device blurring for field photos.
  • Access control: role-scoped retrieval AND column-level SQL policy — two layers, never one; exec corpora segregated at storage; quarterly injection/exfiltration red-teams. demonstrated
  • Human-in-the-loop tiers: Assist (drafts/forecasts) → Augment (human approves each action) → Automate (calibrated confidence gate, named failing checks, override queue). Promotion only by council sign-off on measured error rates.
  • Model risk: model cards, pre-release backtests vs incumbent, bias checks where people are affected, drift monitors, challengers, kill-switch + manual fallback.
  • Audit: immutable decision logs (input, model version, confidence, rule, human, outcome); overrides tracked and fed back; annual independent audit of Tier-3 systems; Sev-1/2/3 incident taxonomy with runbooks.
  • Ownership: every production system has a named business owner and technical owner — no orphan models.
Aligned to DPDP Act 2023 · NIST AI RMF · ISO/IEC 42001 vocabulary

The ask

Three decisions unlock Phase 0

  1. Sponsor it. Name an executive sponsor; convene the AI Council in month 0. Governance is a precondition, not paperwork.
  2. Open the data. A two-week audit across DMS/ERP extracts replaces every major estimate with actuals and re-scores the portfolio before serious money moves.
  3. Fund Phase 0: ₹0.9–1.4 cr. Three shadow-mode pilots — copilot, document automation, demand cockpit — judged only against pre-agreed exit gates. If the gates fail, the programme stops cheaply; if they pass, the ₹6-cr month-12 bar is already in sight.
